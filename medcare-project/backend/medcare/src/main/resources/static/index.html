About MedCare

Delivering compassionate, world-class healthcare to communities across Tamil Nadu since 2008.

Our Story

15+ Years of Trusted Healthcare

Founded in 2008, MedCare Multi-Speciality Clinic began with a simple vision: to make expert medical care accessible, affordable, and compassionate for every patient who walks through our doors.

Today, we have grown into one of Tamil Nadu's most trusted healthcare institutions, serving over 15,000 patients annually with a team of 25+ specialist doctors and 100+ support staff across 12 specialities.

🎯 Our Mission

To provide compassionate, evidence-based, and patient-centred healthcare that improves the quality of life for every individual and family we serve.

🔭 Our Vision

To be the most trusted multi-speciality healthcare provider in South India, recognized for clinical excellence, innovation, and compassionate care.

General Physician

Pediatrics

Specialized care for children from birth to 18 years. Our child-friendly environment ensures a comfortable experience.

  • Newborn care & vaccination
  • Growth monitoring
  • Developmental assessment
  • Child nutrition counselling

Diabetes Care

Comprehensive diabetes management programme addressing blood sugar control, diet, and complication prevention.

  • Blood glucose management
  • HbA1c monitoring
  • Diet & lifestyle coaching
  • Complication screening

Women's Health

Complete gynecological and obstetric care for women at every stage of life, delivered with privacy and compassion.

  • Menopause care

Diagnostics Lab

NABL-certified in-house laboratory with digital results, rapid processing, and home sample collection services.
